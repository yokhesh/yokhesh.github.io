About Me

My expertise in robotics, computer vision and machine learning has been pivotal in advancing the efficacy and accuracy of various tasks involved in healthcare, infrastructure (automated robot-assisted building maintenance and monitoring) and search & rescue applications. The seamless integration of virtual reality with rehabilitation robots during my tenure at CSTAR has significantly enhanced diagnostic tools for neurological disorders. Our team’s efforts have led to the development of a rehabilitation robots and deep neural network model, contributing to more effective patient assessments. Furthermore, I have developed robot system equipped with cutting-edge custom-built computer vision algorithm for infrastructure maintenance and monitoring. With competencies in C# and PLC, honed through hands-on experience in designing neural network-based fault detection algorithms, I am committed to leading innovation in the robotics field. The recognition through the publication of several research articles and editor’s choice article awards reflects the impact of my research, and I am motivated to continue contributing to transformative robotic solutions.
